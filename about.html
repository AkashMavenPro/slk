About SLK Traders

Established in 1952, SLK Traders is a third-generation manufacturing company that began as a rolling mill and has grown into a complete manufacturing solution provider. We offer total solutions of casting, fabrication, and machining under one roof.

Our manufacturing facility in Howrah, West Bengal is equipped with modern infrastructure to deliver tailor-made products as per customer specifications and engineering drawings. We work with a wide range of ferrous and non-ferrous metals including Cast Iron, Steel, Stainless Steel, Brass, Bronze, and Gun Metal.

We supply to reputed organizations across India with consistent quality, timely delivery, and reliable performance.

Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver high-quality steel products and castings that meet exact specifications and international standards, ensuring reliability, precision, and complete customer satisfaction.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be synonymous with excellence and reliability in casting and metal products through innovation, experience, and professional service, while fostering a safe and growth-oriented work environment.
